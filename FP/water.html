The Water

Individual Work

Time: 06/2020-07/2020

Duration: 5 weeks

Material: Iron Wire, Plastic Film, Optical Fiber, Pencil Sketch on Paper

×

The sculpture representing a stream flowing out of a box

Size: 14 x 14 x 14 in.

Inspired by " ... and its frail image just disappeared, like water in water‚Äù by Jorge Luis Borges, I try to explore the relationship between water and life through this project. Curving streams of wires and plastic films flow out of an iron frame, suggesting that life is like water, which could go outside of box. Meanwhile, one of the streams forms a closed loop, symbolizing that life, like water, is cyclical.

The Digital Collage of the Sculpture

The Conceptual Pencil Sketch

Size: 21 x 15 in.

I hope to emphasize the fluidity of the water. Inspired by Cubism, I put four different perspectives of the model in the same picture, and combine their water streams together to create a sense of flow as a unity.
