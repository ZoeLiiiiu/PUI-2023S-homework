Mobile Home

Academic Project

Individual Work

Class: Architecture Design Studio 2

Time: 3/2022-5/2022

Duration: 7 weeks

Instructor: Zaid Kashef Alghata

Software Used: Rhino, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Keyshot, Twinmotion, Adobe Premiere

Triptychs 1: The Mobile Home Units Seperate

Triptychs 2: The Reunion

Triptychs 3: The Two Units Combine Together

The Triptychs showing the seperation and reunion of the mobile home units

In the project, I want to emphasize the space flexibility of the mobile home. I believe a house need to be able to fold into a small part to be mobile, and a house needs to have adequate space to function as a home. Therefore, I focus on how space folds and extends.

I also explore the reconstruction of the family structure. In one of my iterations, I create a splitable house ---- a house that can be split into two parts if needed. This structure aims to solve problems related to family separation, such as the split of the communal property when divorced.
